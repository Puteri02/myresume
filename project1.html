Whenever people goes out for vacation, they tend to get worried about their plants because most plants needs water in daily basis. Thus, this project aimed to satisfy the plant owners by keeping them at ease by having the soil moisture sensor to auto detect when the plants need watering. The plants will be watered by water pump that is generated by the 5V DC motor.


Materials Used

  • Arduino Uno
  • Breadboard
  • Jumper wires
  • Relay
  • 5V DC motor
  • Diode 1N4007
  • Soil Moisture Sensor
  • Resistor 221ohm
